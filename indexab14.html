RANCH PARTY POSTER

Project Details

A poster I did while working at OSI at UCF. I was given the opportunity to design a poster for the Late Knights outdoor event “Ranch Party”. For this poster I decided to give it a more social feel by illustrating two mason jars being brought together in a dynamic (Non-alcoholic) cheers.
